What is JEE Advanced 2027?

JEE Advanced (Joint Entrance Examination — Advanced) is the second tier of the JEE system in India. It is a highly challenging, national-level engineering entrance exam conducted by one of the seven zonal Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) under the guidance of the Joint Admission Board (JAB). Only the top 2,50,000 qualifiers of JEE Main are eligible to sit for JEE Advanced. Qualifying this examination is the sole criteria for admission to undergraduate programs leading to Bachelor's, Integrated Master's, or Bachelor-Master Dual Degrees in engineering, sciences, and architecture at all the 23 IITs.

JEE Advanced 2027 consists of two compulsory papers (Paper 1 and Paper 2) conducted on the same day. Candidates must appear in both papers to qualify for the rank list. Eligibility Criteria

To sit for the JEE Advanced 2027 exam, candidates must satisfy all five criteria set by the Joint Admission Board. Failure to meet any eligibility clause leads to immediate disqualification.

Performance in JEE Main

  • Must rank within the top 2,50,000 unique candidates in JEE Main 2027 Paper 1.
  • Category-wise shares: Gen (10.1%), GEN-EWS (10%), OBC-NCL (27%), SC (15%), ST (7.5%), PwD (5% horizontal in each).

Age Limit & Attempts

  • Born on or after October 1, 2002.
  • SC, ST, and PwD candidates get a 5-year relaxation (born on or after October 1, 1997).
  • Maximum of 2 attempts in consecutive years.
Criterion Detailed Requirements
Class 12 Appearance Must have appeared for 10+2 examinations for the first time in either 2026 or 2027. Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics are mandatory.
Prior IIT Admission Must not have been admitted to any IIT program previously, nor accepted an IIT seat by reporting online or at a reporting center. Candidates whose admission at IIT was canceled are also ineligible.

OCI/PIO and Foreign National Candidates

  • As per the Supreme Court ruling, OCI/PIO cards obtained before March 4, 2021 are treated as Indian nationals and qualify through the standard categories.
  • Foreign national candidates (non-OCI/PIO cardholders) do not receive reservation quotas. Up to 10% supernumerary seats are allocated for international students.
  • All international students studying in India must qualify for the JEE Advanced via the standard JEE Main ranking path.

Registration & Application Process

The application portal for JEE Advanced 2027 opens soon after the announcement of JEE Main results. The process must be completed online via `jeeadv.ac.in`.

Step 1
Log in to the Portal
Access the JEE Advanced registration portal using your JEE Main 2027 Roll Number and Password. Verify candidate parameters retrieved automatically from NTA databases.
Step 2
Fill Additional Fields & Cities
Input additional fields including mailing details. Select 8 preferred examination cities from the provided list. Choose exam medium (English or Hindi).
Step 3
Upload Certificates
Upload clear scanned copies (PDF files, between 50 KB and 300 KB) of mandatory documents. Refer below for document specifications.
Step 4
Pay Fees & Print Confirmation
Complete registration fee payment online. Download and print the confirmation page for JoSAA verification records.

Required Scanned Documents (PDF, 50KB to 300KB)

  • Class X Birth Certificate or Equivalent
  • Class XII (or Equivalent Examination) Certificate
  • Category Certificate (OBC-NCL/SC/ST/GEN-EWS)
  • PwD Certificate / Scribe Request Letter (if applicable)
  • DS Certificate (Defense Service personnel)
  • OCI/PIO Card (if applicable)

Registration Fees

JEE Advanced Syllabus

The syllabus outlines are based on standard NCERT formats but have deeper conceptual applications. Zonal IITs release syllabus booklets alongside the information brochure.

Physics

  • Mechanics (Kinematics, Newton's Laws)
  • Thermal Physics (Thermodynamics, Calorimetry)
  • SHM and Waves (Wave Optics, Sound waves)
  • Electricity and Magnetism (Electrostatics, EMI)
  • Optics (Ray Optics, Reflection, Refraction)
  • Modern Physics (Atomic nucleus, Photoelectric effect)
  • Thermal Physics & Properties of Solids/Fluids

Chemistry

  • Physical Chemistry (Atomic structure, Kinetics)
  • Inorganic Chemistry (Coordination compounds, p-block)
  • Organic Chemistry (Basic principles, Hydrocarbons)
  • Mole Concept & Gaseous State
  • Chemical Bonding & Molecular Structure
  • Metallurgy, Qualitative analysis, Biomolecules
  • Alkanes, Alkenes, Alkynes, Ketones, Carboxylic acids

Mathematics

  • Algebra (Quadratic, Complex, Probability)
  • Vectors & 3D Analytical Geometry
  • Matrices & Determinants
  • Integral Calculus (Definite, Area curves)
  • Differential Calculus (Limits, Applications)
  • Trigonometry & Inverse Functions
  • Probability & Combinatorics

IIT Roorkee has released the detailed syllabus mapping. Check the complete subject guides: JEE Advanced Syllabus 2027 for comprehensive topic lists and weightage allocations.

JoSAA Counselling - Admission to IITs

Once rank standings are declared, standard online counselling is coordinated by the Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA). JoSAA registration and online choice-locking begins in the first week of June 2027. Qualified candidates list their branches in decreasing order of preference.

Seat Allotment Steps

  • JoSAA portal registration with JEE Main credentials.
  • Online choice filling of colleges and branches.
  • Mock allotment rounds to understand choice trends.
  • Online Seat Acceptance & Fee payment.
  • Online document upload & verification processes.

Participating IIT Zones

  • IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, IIT Madras, IIT Kanpur
  • IIT Kharagpur, IIT Roorkee, IIT Guwahati
  • Newer IITs: IIT Hyderabad, IIT Indore, IIT Ropar, etc.
  • Total of 23 Indian Institutes of Technology participating.
