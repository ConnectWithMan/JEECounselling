1.0 Overview: JEE Main 2027 Registration

The National Testing Agency (NTA) will open the online JEE Main 2027 Registration process starting in October 2026 for the January Session (Session 1). Candidates can complete their application form exclusively by visiting the official NTA web portal: jeemain.nta.nic.in. Completing registration accurately is necessary for obtaining the NTA admit card, checking JEE Main Syllabus requirements, and qualifying for JEE Advanced Registration and JoSAA Counselling.

NTA also releases a demo registration link, detailed application instruction bulletin, and final examination schedule on the portal prior to activation. Aspirants are advised to monitor the official portal closely for live notifications and review the JEE Main Exam Pattern.

Registration Highlights:
• Session 1: Online registration starts in October 2026.
• Session 2: Registration for April Session 2 opens in February 2027.
• Core Stages: 1) Registration & Credentials, 2) Filling Form Details & Document Upload, 3) Online Fee Payment.
• Session Options: Candidates can choose to apply for Session 1, Session 2, or both sessions together.
• Multiple Forms Warning: Submitting multiple application forms for the same session by a candidate will lead to immediate rejection of all submissions.

3.0 Why You Should Update Your Documents Before JEE Main 2027 Registration

Many students encounter critical errors during registration due to mismatched or outdated personal details in their official identity documents. Discrepancies in:

  • Candidate Name spelling variations
  • Date of Birth (DOB) mismatches between Aadhaar & Class 10 certificate
  • Address changes or expired Category/Reservation certificates
  • Outdated photographs on Aadhaar identity cards

Such mismatch errors can result in JEE Main application rejection or unnecessary complications during JoSAA physical verification. Check official JEE Main Reservation Policy and JEE Main Cutoff criteria to verify category certificate guidelines. NTA strictly advises candidates to review and update their Aadhaar and identity certificates well before registration begins.

Essential Document Verification Checklist:
1. Aadhaar Card: Name and Date of Birth must match Class 10 Marksheet exactly. Update latest photo and father's name.
2. UDID Card (for PwD Candidates): Ensure the Unique Disability ID card is valid and renewed if expired.
3. Category Certificate: EWS, OBC-NCL, SC, or ST certificates must be issued in valid government formats by authorized officers.

5.0 Step-by-Step JEE Main 2027 Registration Process

Follow this step-by-step procedure to complete your NTA application form smoothly:

Step 1
NTA Account Registration & Credentials Generation
Visit jeemain.nta.nic.in. Click on "New Candidate Register Here". Enter your name, father's name, mother's name, DOB, valid mobile number, and email address. Create a strong security password to generate your 12-digit Application Number.
Step 2
Filling Up Online Application Details
Log in using your Application Number and Password. Fill in personal details, nationality, category, state code of eligibility, Class 10 & 12 board marks, school address, and year of passing.
Step 3
Selecting Exam Paper & Choice of 4 Cities
Select exam papers (B.E./B.Tech, B.Arch, or B.Planning), exam medium (English, Hindi, Assamese, Bengali, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Odia, Punjabi, Tamil, Telugu, Urdu), and choose 4 preferred examination cities.
Step 4
Document Upload
Upload clear scanned passport photograph, candidate signature, and category/PwD certificates strictly maintaining NTA file size constraints (Photo: 10-200 KB, Sign: 4-30 KB).
Step 5
Application Fee Payment Online
Pay the non-refundable registration fee using Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI gateway (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Canara, Paytm). Verify transaction status.
Step 6
Review & Print Confirmation Page
Thoroughly review all details on screen before final submission. Download, save, and print at least 3 physical copies of the NTA Confirmation Page for counselling reference.
