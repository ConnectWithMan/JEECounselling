JEE Main 2027 Session 1 (January) Important Dates

JEE Main Exam Shift Timings & Schedule

JEE Main is administered across two daily shifts. Candidates must strictly adhere to reporting times and gate closure rules printed on their admit cards.

Paper / Course Shift Reporting Time Gate Closing Time Exam Duration
Paper 1 (B.E. / B.Tech) Shift 1 (Morning) 07:30 AM – 08:30 AM 08:30 AM 09:00 AM to 12:00 PM (3 Hrs)
Shift 2 (Afternoon) 01:30 PM – 02:30 PM 02:30 PM 03:00 PM to 06:00 PM (3 Hrs)
Paper 2A (B.Arch) Single Shift / Shift 1 07:30 AM – 08:30 AM 08:30 AM 09:00 AM to 12:30 PM (3.5 Hrs)
Paper 2B (B.Planning) Single Shift / Shift 2 01:30 PM – 02:30 PM 02:30 PM 03:00 PM to 06:30 PM (3.5 Hrs)
Both Paper 2A & 2B Combined Combined Shift 07:30 AM – 08:30 AM 08:30 AM 09:00 AM to 01:00 PM (4 Hrs)

Gate Closing Warning: NTA prohibits candidate entry into the exam hall after 08:30 AM (Shift 1) and 02:30 PM (Shift 2). Arrive early to complete biometric and Aadhaar verification smoothly.
