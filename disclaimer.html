2. College Predictor Accuracy & Probabilistic Nature

Our college prediction algorithms compute seat probabilities based on public opening and closing ranks published in previous JoSAA and CSAB counselling years.

While we update our database after every round, actual closing ranks vary every year due to changes in student preferences, seat matrix revisions, candidate performance variations, and category shifts. Predictions generated on this site do NOT guarantee seat allotment or admission into any college.
