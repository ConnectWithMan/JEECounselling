1. Information We Collect

At JEE Counselling, we collect minimal data necessary to provide accurate college predictions and a seamless user experience:

  • Calculator Inputs: When using our College Predictors or Percentile Tools, inputs such as rank, category, quota, or preferred branch are processed in real-time in your browser session and are not stored in any user tracking database.
  • Contact Form Data: If you reach out to us via our Contact Us form, we collect your name, email address, subject, and message details solely to process your support request.
  • Automated Technical Logs: standard non-identifiable web statistics like browser type, device type, referring URL, time spent, and anonymized IP addresses are logged for network diagnostic purposes.
Our Privacy Commitment:

We never sell, trade, rent, or lease any student personal data or contact details to third-party telemarketers, coaching institutes, or private colleges.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information gathered on jeecounselling.com is used strictly for:

  • Calculating real-time B.Tech admission probabilities for IITs, NITs, IIITs, and GFTIs.
  • Responding to student inquiries, technical bug reports, and user feedback.
  • Improving page performance, site speed, navigation layout, and content readability.
  • Preventing malicious bot activities and ensuring cyber security.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website utilizes standard cookies to optimize your browsing experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform functionality, navigation, and theme rendering.
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymized performance metrics (e.g. via Google Analytics) helping us understand which cutoff pages and guides are most useful to students.

You can choose to disable cookies in your individual browser settings; however, certain interactive predictor features may experience minor navigation changes.

4. Third-Party Services & Links

Our site contains links to official authority portals (e.g., NTA jeemain.nta.nic.in, JoSAA josaa.nic.in, CSAB csab.nic.in) and official community links (e.g. WhatsApp channel updates).

Please note that once you navigate away from jeecounselling.com, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We recommend reviewing the privacy practices of any third-party destination site.

5. Aspirant Data Rights & Inquiries

You have the full right to ask about the personal data you have submitted via our support forms, request data deletion, or unsubscribe from any updates.

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, please write to us directly at our Contact Page.
