JEE Advanced Shift Hours & Exam Day Schedule

JEE Advanced consists of two compulsory papers conducted on the same day. Candidates must appear for BOTH Paper 1 and Paper 2 according to the official JEE Advanced syllabus; failing to attend either paper results in automatic disqualification. Qualified candidates will proceed to JoSAA counselling for IIT seat allocation.

Paper Name Shift Reporting Time Gate Closing Exam Duration
Paper 1 Forenoon Shift (Morning) 07:00 AM IST 08:30 AM IST 09:00 AM to 12:00 PM (3 Hours)
Paper 2 Afternoon Shift (Evening) 01:00 PM IST 02:00 PM IST 02:30 PM to 05:30 PM (3 Hours)

Compulsory Rule: Evaluation for Paper 2 occurs ONLY if a candidate has taken Paper 1. Final aggregate marks and All India Ranks are computed only for candidates who complete both papers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - JEE Advanced Dates

JEE Advanced 2027 is expected to be conducted on Sunday, May 16, 2027. Both Paper 1 and Paper 2 are held on the same day.
Paper 1 runs from 09:00 AM to 12:00 PM IST (Morning Shift). Paper 2 runs from 02:30 PM to 05:30 PM IST (Afternoon Shift). Candidates must attend both shifts.
Response sheets are uploaded to candidate portals around May 20, 2027. Provisional answer keys are published on May 24, 2027 with a 24 to 48-hour objection window.
JEE Advanced 2027 results and rank lists will be declared on Monday, May 31, 2027 at 10:00 AM IST on the official web portal jeeadv.ac.in.
JoSAA counselling registration and choice filling begin immediately on the next day, June 01, 2027 at 05:00 PM IST.
