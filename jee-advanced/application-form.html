Overview: JEE Advanced 2027 Application Form

The JEE Advanced 2027 Application Form is the official online gateway that candidates must complete to appear for the prestigious Joint Entrance Examination (Advanced). It is managed by the organizing IIT (IIT Delhi for 2027) on the official website. Access is strictly limited to candidates who have qualified in JEE Main 2027 (Paper 1) and placed in the top 2,50,000 unique merit ranks across categories.

The registration process includes importing credentials from the NTA JEE Main system, specifying exam centers, uploading academic certificates in PDF format, paying the non-refundable registration fee, and keeping the final receipt safe for JoSAA counselling verification rounds.

Important Rule: Candidates must attempt both Paper 1 and Paper 2 on the exam day. Missing either shift automatically results in disqualification and cancellation of evaluation.

Eligibility Criteria to Apply

Candidates must fulfill all 5 eligibility criteria set by the conducting IIT to apply for JEE Advanced 2027:

Parameters Requirements
1. JEE Main Performance Must be among the top 2,50,000 candidates in the Paper 1 (B.Tech) combined merit list across categories in JEE Main 2027.
2. Age Limit General, EWS, and OBC candidates must be born on or after October 1, 2002. SC, ST, and PwD candidates get a 5-year relaxation (born on or after October 1, 1997).
3. Number of Attempts Allowed a maximum of 2 times in 2 consecutive years.
4. Class 12 Year Must have appeared for the Class 12 board exam for the first time in either 2026 or 2027. Attempting Class 12 in 2025 or earlier makes you ineligible.
5. No Prior IIT Admission Must never have been admitted or accepted seat allotment at any IIT previously. Candidates who withdrew before classes started are eligible.

For the category-wise distribution of the 2,50,000 qualifying pool, refer to our detailed JEE Advanced Eligibility Guide.

Required Documents for Upload

Unlike JEE Main, most certificates for JEE Advanced must be uploaded as PDF scans between 50 KB and 300 KB. Keep the following files ready:

  • Class 10 mark sheet or birth certificate (for DOB verification).
  • Class 12 board mark sheet (for those who passed in 2026).
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC-NCL/Gen-EWS): Ensure it is issued in the official format after April 1 of the exam year. Refer to our JEE Advanced Reservation Rules.
  • PwD Certificate (if applicable, for candidates seeking reservation).
  • Scribe Request Letter (if applicable, for physically disabled candidates).
  • OCI or PIO Card / Passport scan (for foreign national applicants).

How to Fill the JEE Advanced 2027 Application Form?

Step 1
Log in on jeeadv.ac.in
Access the portal. Enter your NTA JEE Main Application Number and password to log in. The system will retrieve your basic credentials, scores, and personal data.
Step 2
Fill Additional Fields & City Choices
Specify details such as language of question paper (English/Hindi), scribe requirement status, and select up to 8 preferred exam cities from available zones.
Step 3
Upload Certificates (PDF Format)
Upload clear scanned copies of birth certificate, qualifying marksheet, and category declarations. Check sizes (50 KB - 300 KB) before uploading.
Step 4
Pay the Registration Fees
Pay the non-refundable registration fee online. Check the official schedule below for correct payment categories.
Step 5
Download Registration Confirmation
Verify all details on the final preview. Submit and download the final registration receipt. Keep it safe for physical reporting rounds.

Post-Submission: What is Next?

After successfully submitting your application form, prepare for the subsequent milestones:

  1. Admit Card Download: Download the hall ticket from the portal about one week before the exam. Inspect test center location, reporting times, and instructions.
  2. The Examination: Appear for both Paper 1 (9 AM - 12 PM) and Paper 2 (2:30 PM - 5:30 PM) on the exam day.
  3. Responses Display & Challenges: The IIT releases scanned candidate response sheets and provisional answer keys. Candidates can submit challenges for key rectifications.
  4. Results & JoSAA: Final keys and ranks are released. JoSAA counselling commences within 2-3 days for choice filling at 23 IITs.
