1.0 Overview: JEE Advanced 2027 Registration

The JEE Advanced 2027 Registration is the official online application process that candidates qualifying JEE Main (Paper 1) must complete to appear for the Indian Institutes of Technology (IIT) entrance examination. The registration portal is exclusively operated at jeeadv.ac.in by the organizing IIT. Completing registration is necessary for downloading your JEE Advanced Admit Card, filling out the JEE Advanced Application Form, and participating in JoSAA Counselling.

Only candidates who secure a rank among the top 2,50,000 in JEE Main across all categories and satisfy all 5 mandatory eligibility conditions can log in, upload documents, pay the registration fee, and prepare according to the JEE Advanced Syllabus and JEE Advanced Exam Pattern.

Official Portal Notice: The only authorized link for JEE Advanced registration is jeeadv.ac.in. Candidates log in using their JEE Main Registration Roll Number and Password to automatically import personal data and eligibility records.

3.0 Who Can Apply for JEE Advanced 2027? (5 Mandatory Criteria)

Before filling the application form, every candidate must confirm they fulfill all 5 eligibility criteria set by the conducting IIT. Failing even one condition cancels eligibility regardless of JEE Main score:

Criteria # Eligibility Condition Detailed Requirement
Criterion 1 JEE Main Performance Must rank among top 2,50,000 successful candidates in B.E./B.Tech (Paper 1) across combined category merit lists. Check JEE Main Qualifying Cutoff.
Criterion 2 Age Limit General/OBC/EWS candidates born on or after October 1, 2002. SC/ST/PwD candidates get 5-year relaxation (born on/after October 1, 1997).
Criterion 3 Number of Attempts Maximum of 2 attempts allowed in 2 consecutive years only.
Criterion 4 Class 12 Passing Year Must have appeared in Class 12 (or equivalent) for the first time in 2026 or 2027 with Physics, Chemistry, & Mathematics.
Criterion 5 No Prior IIT Admission Must not have been admitted to any IIT previously, or had seat cancelled after reporting.

4.0 Required Documents for JEE Advanced Registration

Keep scanned PDF/JPEG copies of the following documents ready before logging into jeeadv.ac.in. Refer to the JEE Advanced Reservation Policy for official certificate formats:

  • Class 10 Certificate / Birth Certificate: For Date of Birth verification.
  • Class 12 Marksheet / Qualifying Examination Certificate: Showing Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics subjects.
  • Category Certificate (if applicable): SC, ST, OBC-NCL, or Gen-EWS certificate issued in the prescribed format on or after April 1 of the financial year.
  • PwD Certificate / UDID Card: Issued by authorized medical board for candidates requesting scribe or time relaxation.
  • OCI / PIO Citizenship Certificate: For foreign national candidates or OCI cardholders.
  • Scanned Photograph & Signature: High clarity image adhering to official dimensions.

5.0 How to Fill the JEE Advanced Application Form? (7 Steps)

Step 1
Visit Official Portal
Navigate to jeeadv.ac.in during the application window announced post JEE Main results.
Step 2
Login with JEE Main Credentials
Enter your JEE Main 2027 Application Number, Password, and Date of Birth to log into the candidate portal.
Step 3
Verify Imported Data & Choose Exam Cities
Verify pre-filled personal and academic details. Select examination city choices (up to 4 cities), question paper language (English/Hindi), and scribe options.
Step 4
Upload Certificates & Documents
Upload clear scanned copies of Class 10/12 marksheets, Category/PwD certificates, and photos as required.
Step 5
Pay Online Application Fee
Pay the non-refundable registration fee using Net Banking, Credit Card, Debit Card, or UPI online payment gateways.
Step 6
Application Form Preview & Submission
Review all submitted data carefully. Click final submit to complete registration.
Step 7
Print Confirmation Receipt
Download and print the final registration confirmation receipt. Keep the application number safe for admit card download and JoSAA counselling reporting.

7.0 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - JEE Advanced Registration

Applications for JEE Advanced 2027 are scheduled to open in April 2027 immediately following the declaration of JEE Main 2027 results.
To complete the JEE Advanced application form, candidates should visit the official website: jeeadv.ac.in.
The process begins with candidates registering on jeeadv.ac.in, logging in with JEE Main credentials, uploading requisite certificates, making online fee payment, and saving the confirmation receipt.
The exam is conducted in Computer Based Test (CBT) online mode. Candidates must appear for both compulsory Paper 1 and Paper 2 on the same day.
The application fee for female candidates (all categories) is ₹1,600.
