Our Mission & Core Vision

Every year, over 14 lakh engineering aspirants appear for JEE Main and JEE Advanced in India. While scoring high marks is a monumental achievement, navigating JoSAA (Joint Seat Allocation Authority) and CSAB (Central Seat Allocation Board) counselling procedures can be equally challenging and intimidating.

JEE Counselling was founded with a singular mission: to eliminate ambiguity from college admissions by offering transparent, data-backed seat allocation predictions and choice filling guidance completely free of charge.

Our Core Philosophy:

We believe every student deserves equal access to accurate admission metrics, opening & closing rank trends, and branch selection insights without expensive agent consultation fees.

Why Students Trust JEE Counselling

Our Data Engine & Analytics Methodology

Multi-Parameter Evaluation

Unlike simple static cutoff charts, our prediction engine accounts for complex allocation rules:

  • Quota Rules: Home State (HS) vs. Other State (OS) and All India (AI) seats.
  • Reservation Pool: Category ranks for OPEN, EWS, OBC-NCL, SC, ST, and PwD.
  • Gender Pool: Gender-Neutral vs. Female-Only supernumerary quotas.

Round-by-Round Trend Archiving

We archive historical opening and closing rank statistics across all official counselling rounds to show you true seat movement patterns and cutoff variations.
